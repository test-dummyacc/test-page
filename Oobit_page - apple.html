Questions?

How do I use Oobit?
Using Oobit is easy:

Download the App: Install it from your app store.
Set Up Your Account: Register and link your crypto wallet.
Start Paying: Pay with Oobit anywhere Visa and Mastercard are accepted for seamless crypto-to-fiat transactions.
How secure is Oobit?
When you pay with Oobit in-store, your privacy is fully protected. Our use of native NFC (Near field communication) technology ensures your data is encrypted and secure, keeping every transaction private and seamless.
How do I set up Oobit?
Setting up Oobit is quick and simple:

Download the App: Get Oobit from your app store.
Create Your Account: Sign up with your details and verify your identity.
Link Your Wallet: Connect your crypto wallet to start using Oobit.
I don’t have a crypto wallet. Can I still use Oobit?
Absolutely! Oobit is designed for everyone, whether you have a crypto wallet or not. You can buy crypto instantly using your bank card, store it securely in your Oobit account, and start making payments right away—just like a regular mobile payment app. No prior crypto experience needed!
